My Favorite Banana Bread Recipe!

I found this recipe on Pintrest and I added some tweaks to it.

What you will need:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 ripe bananas (the more ripe the better!)
  • One egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (or measure to your heart!)
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  • Optional: chocolate, walnuts, (to your liking!) and/or 1 extra banana for decoration.

Directions:

  1. On low to medium heat, put 1/2 cup of unsalted into the pot until it is bubbling and is now brown.
  2. Now mix, after cooling for atleast 10 min, the brown butter and 1 cup brown sugar until fully combined.
  3. Mash your 3 ripe bananas.
  4. In the same bowl with the brown sugar and brown butter mixture, mix in the 3 mashed banans until fully incorporated.
  5. Next, add 1 egg the 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (or, again, measure to your heart!) and mix.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix your dry ingredients until fully combined:
    • 2 cups flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  7. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (depending on how hot your stove can get, this may vary.
  8. In the bowl with wet ingredients, fold in the dry ingredients until fully combined.
  9. *Optional* Next, mix in your choclate and/or walnuts.
  10. Line your baking pan with parchment paper and make sure some is sticking up so it can be easy to take out when done baking.
  11. Put the batter into the pan. *Optional* slice a banana right down the middle into two slices or into small slices to decorate the top.
  12. Put it into the oven for 30-45 min, MAX!
  13. Once done, let it sit for 5-10 min.
  14. Enjoy!!!
